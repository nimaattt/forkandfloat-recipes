dietaryTags: "Veg, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Contains Nuts, Seasonal"
ingredients:
  - name: "Pitter olives"
    amount: "3 cups"
  - name: "Walnuts (grated)"
    amount: "3/4 cup"
  - name: "Cilantro (finely chopped)"
    amount: "1/3 cup"
  - name: "Parsley (finely chopped)"
    amount: "1/2"
  - name: "Mint(finely chopped)"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Garlic (minced)"
    amount: "1-2 cloves"
  - name: "Pomegranate Molases"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Pomegranate Seeds"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Fresh Pomegranate juice"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Olive oil"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Sat and Pepper"
    amount: "To taste"
  - name: "Ground Persian hogweed (Golpar)"
    amount: "1 tbsp —— optional"
steps:
  - title: "Prepare the herb base"
    body: "Add cilantro, parsley, mint, and garlic to a mixer and finely mince everything together.(Alternatively, chop everything very finely by hand.)"
  - title: "Grind the walnuts"
    body: "Finely grind or grate the walnuts until they reach a soft, crumb-like texture."
  - title: "Combine everything"
    body: "In a large bowl, mix the olives, herb mixture, walnuts, olive oil, pomegranate molasses, and pomegranate seeds as well as the pomegranate juice, then season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve Mix until fully coated, serve fresh or store in the fridge for 24 hours to rest for best result."
howToServe:
  - "Can be served immediately but for best result store for 24 hours in the fridge. Gets better over time!"
  - "Serve as appetizer or as a side dish with grilled meats or fish, rice dishes or simply with bread"
chefsNote: "If your olives are too salty, soak them in water overnight to draw out some of the saltiness. Drain well before using. Go for large, firm green olives. They hold shape best over time. This dish should hit salty, tangy, and slightly sweet all at once . Adjust pomegranate molasses to taste. Keep everything finely chopped, but not pasty.  You want a bit of texture from the walnuts and herbs."

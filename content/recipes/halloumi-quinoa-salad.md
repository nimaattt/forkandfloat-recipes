emoji: "🍽️"
dietaryTags: "Veg, Gluten-Free"
ingredients:
  - name: "Quinoa"
    amount: "1 1/2  cup"
  - name: "Halloumi Cheese"
    amount: "1 block"
  - name: "Asparagus"
    amount: "Half a bunch"
  - name: "Mint leaves (chopped)"
    amount: "1/4 cup"
  - name: "Zucchini"
    amount: "1 large"
  - name: "Lime"
    amount: "1"
  - name: "Lime zest"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Grainy mustard"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Ex-V Olive oil"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Nutritional yeast (optional)"
    amount: "1/4 cup"
  - name: "Maple Syrup"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Salt and Pepper"
    amount: "To taste"
steps:
  - title: "Prep the ingredients"
    body: "Cut the halloumi into 1-inch triangle pieces (or smaller if you prefer). Slice the asparagus into 2-inch diagonal pieces. Use a vegetable peeler to shave the zucchini into long, thin strips all the way to the core. Roughly chop the fresh mint, setting a small amount aside for garnish."
  - title: "Cook the quinoa"
    body: "Rinse the quinoa and add it to a pot with equal parts water (e.g., 1 cup quinoa to 1 cup water). Bring to a boil on high heat, then reduce to medium-low and simmer until the water is absorbed and the grains appear slightly larger and fluffy."
  - title: "Cook the halloumi and asparagus"
    body: "Heat a dry pan over medium heat (no oil). Add the halloumi pieces and cook, flipping every couple of minutes, until all sides are golden and crispy. Remove from the pan. Add a drizzle of oil to the same pan and sauté the asparagus with a pinch of salt and pepper for about 1 minute, just until vibrant green. Remove from heat."
  - title: "Make the dressing"
    body: "In a small cup or bowl, whisk together: mustard, lime juice, lime zest, olive oil, maple syrup, and nutritional yeast until smooth and well combined."
  - title: "Assemble the salad"
    body: "In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, zucchini strips, chopped mint, sautéed asparagus, and crispy halloumi. Pour the dressing over and toss gently to combine."
  - title: "Serve"
    body: "Plate the salad and top with reserved mint and freshly ground black pepper."
howToServe: []
chefsNote: "Best served fresh, but can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 days."

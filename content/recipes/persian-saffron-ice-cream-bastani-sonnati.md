emoji: "🍽️"
dietaryTags: "Veg, Gluten-Free, Contains Nuts"
ingredients:
  - name: "Vanilla Ice Cream"
    amount: "1.5 L"
  - name: "Saffron (Ground And Bloomed)"
    amount: "½ Tsp"
  - name: "Rose Water"
    amount: "2 Tbsp"
  - name: "Salted Pistachios (Finely Chopped)"
    amount: "½ Cup"
  - name: "Heavy Cream (Frozen And Chopped)"
    amount: "½ Cup"
  - name: "Dried Rose Petals"
    amount: "From 2 Small Roses"
steps:
  - title: ""
    body: "Leave the ice cream at room temperature for 1–2 hours until softened."
  - title: ""
    body: "Pour the heavy cream into a flat dish and freeze for 2–3 hours until firm. Remove and chop into small chunks using a sharp knife."
  - title: ""
    body: "Finely grind the saffron and bloom it by adding a small amount of hot water. Let it sit for at least 10 minutes."
  - title: ""
    body: "Add the softened ice cream to a large bowl. Add the rose water, bloomed saffron, pistachios, and cream chunks. Gently mix with a spoon or spatula until fully combined."
  - title: ""
    body: "Transfer the mixture to a container and top it off with more chopped pistachios and small pieces of dried rose petals. Then freeze for at least 2 hours until firm."
  - title: ""
    body: "Scoop and serve!"
howToServe:
  - "Serve on its own as a dessert"
  - "Scoop into waffle cones"
  - "Sandwich between waffle crackers for a traditional style"
chefsNote: "Use high-quality saffron for the best flavour and colour (can always find them at persian supermarkets). Adjust the rose water based on your preference Cream chunks are essential for the authentic texture Do not overmix once the cream is added to keep the texture light"

date: 2026-05-29T11:58:00.000Z
emoji: "🍽️"
dietaryTags: "Veg, Gluten-Free, Contains Nuts, Seasonal"
ingredients:
  - name: "Fennel"
    amount: "1 bulb"
  - name: "Gala Apple"
    amount: "1 medium"
  - name: "Walnuts (toasted)"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Shaved Parmesan"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Mustard"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Lime/lemon juice"
    amount: "1 medium"
  - name: "Zest of lime/lemon"
    amount: "1"
  - name: "Extra Virgin Olive Oil"
    amount: "1/4 cup"
  - name: "Honey"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Salt and Pepper"
    amount: "to taste"
steps:
  - title: "Prep the salad"
    body: "Thinly slice the fennel bulb and apple (a mandoline works best for even slices). Finely chop the fennel greens and add them to the bowl. Sprinkle in the lime or lemon zest."
  - title: "Make the dressing"
    body: "In a small jar, combine olive oil, mustard, honey, lime/lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Shake or whisk well until well combined, then pour over the salad mixture. Toss gently to coat."
  - title: "Toast the walnuts"
    body: "Heat a small pan over medium heat. Add the walnuts and stir every minute to prevent burning. Once they turn slightly golden and fragrant, remove from heat and let cool."
  - title: "Plate and serve"
    body: "Arrange the salad on your serving dish. Top with toasted walnuts and shaved parmesan."
howToServe: []
chefsNote: "Best served fresh, but can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 days. Swap walnuts for sunflower seeds if nut-free. You can make this Vegan and Dairy Free simply by replacing Parmesan with  Vegan parmesan."

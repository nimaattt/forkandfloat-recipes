category: "Salads"
tags:

  - vegetarian
prepTime: "10 mins"
cookTime: "15 mins"
servings: "2"
difficulty: "Easy"
coverImage: "/images/uploads/acorn-squash-kale-salad.png"
date: 2026-05-29T11:35:00.000Z
emoji: "🍽️"
dietaryTags: "Veg, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Contains Nuts, Seasonal"
ingredients:
  - name: "Acorn Squash"
    amount: "1 Small"
  - name: "Kale"
    amount: "4 cups (torn)"
  - name: "Pomegranate Seeds"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Roasted almonds (chopped)"
    amount: "1/4 cup"
  - name: "Olive oil"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Lemon (juiced)"
    amount: "1 large"
  - name: "Tahini"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Honey"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Cumin"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Cayenne Pepper (optional)"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Paprika"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Salt and Pepper"
    amount: "to taste"
steps:
  - title: "Roast the squash"
    body: "Cut the acorn squash into thin wedges (so the natural scalloped shape shows). Toss with olive oil, cumin, paprika, cayenne, salt, and black pepper. Roast at 400°F for 15 minutes, or until soft."
  - title: "Prep the kale"
    body: "Tear the kale into small pieces. Add a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt, then massage for 1–2 minutes, until darker, softer, and no longer leathery."
  - title: "Make the dressing"
    body: "Whisk lemon juice, tahini, olive oil, honey, salt, and pepper."
  - title: "Assemble the salad"
    body: "Lay the massaged kale on a plate or wide bowl. Add the warm roasted squash on top. Drizzle with the tahini dressing. Finish with pomegranate seeds and chopped roasted almonds for crunch."
howToServe:
  - "Best Served fresh and while the squash is still warm but can be stored for 2-3 days in the fridge and assemble before serving."
  - "Plate on a wide, shallow bowl so the colors and layers stand out."
chefsNote: "Kale becomes tender and easier to digest once massaged with olive oil and salt. The salad keeps well for meal prep, just add the dressing right before serving."

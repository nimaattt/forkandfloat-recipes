tags:

  - vegetarian
prepTime: "10 mins"
cookTime: "15 mins"
servings: "2"
difficulty: "Easy"
coverImage: "/images/uploads/miso-cauliflower-steak.png"
date: 2026-05-29T11:31:00.000Z
emoji: "🍽️"
dietaryTags: "Veg, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free"
ingredients:
  - name: "Cauliflower"
    amount: "1 medium head"
  - name: "Miso paste"
    amount: "1/4 cup"
  - name: "Olive oil"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Sesame oil"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Honey"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Lemon juice"
    amount: "half a lemon"
  - name: "Tahini"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Parmigiano Reggiano"
    amount: "1/4 cup (freshly grated)"
  - name: "Chives"
    amount: "1/4 cup (finely chopped)"
  - name: "Black Sesame seeds"
    amount: "For garnish"
steps:
  - title: ""
    body: "Slice the cauliflower into thick, flat steaks (about 1-inch thick)."
  - title: ""
    body: "In a small cup, mix miso, sesame oil, olive oil, lemon juice, and honey until it forms a thick sauce."
  - title: ""
    body: "Rub the sauce generously over both sides of the cauliflower slices."
  - title: ""
    body: "Heat a pan over medium-high heat and sear the cauliflower for 2–3 minutes, until one side is lightly browned."
  - title: ""
    body: "Mix the remaining sauce with 1 tbsp of water and pour it into the pan."
  - title: ""
    body: "Cover with a lid and steam for 2–3 minutes, until tender."
  - title: ""
    body: "Remove the lid and transfer the pan to the oven (make sure your pan is oven safe). Roast at 500°F for 5 minutes, until the tops are browned and any liquid has evaporated."
howToServe:
  - "Mix tahini with 1 tbsp honey and spread it on your serving plate."
  - "Place the cauliflower steaks on top."
  - "Finish with freshly grated Parmigiano and a sprinkle of chopped chive and sesame seeds."
chefsNote: "Delicious as a main or side, pairs beautifully with rice, grains, or salad. High in fiber, calcium, and antioxidants. Try adding sesame seeds or chili flakes for extra depth. Best served fresh, but can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 days and warmed up in the oven for best results."

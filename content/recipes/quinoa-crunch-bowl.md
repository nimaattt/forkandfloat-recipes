ingredients:
  - name: "Quinoa"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Kale (chopped)"
    amount: "2 cups"
  - name: "Purple cabbage (thinly sliced)"
    amount: "3/4 cup"
  - name: "Red bell pepper (thinly sliced)"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Cucumber (cubed)"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Avocado (sliced)"
    amount: "1/2 avocado"
  - name: "Tofu (cubed)"
    amount: "One block"
  - name: "Chickpeas (optional, or any preferred protein)"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Peanut Butter"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Sesame oil"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Olive oil"
    amount: "1/2 cup"
  - name: "Rice vinegar"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Siracha (optional)"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Soy sauce"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Lime (juiced)"
    amount: "1/3 cup"
  - name: "Maple syrup or Honey"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Nutritional yeast (optional)"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Ground cumin"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - name: "Turmeric powder"
    amount: "1tsp"
  - name: "Garlic powder"
    amount: "1tsp"
  - name: "Salt & pepper"
    amount: "To taste"
steps:
  - title: "Cook the quinoa"
    body: "Rinse the quinoa and cook it in a small pot with equal parts water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the water is absorbed. Fluff and set aside."
  - title: "Prep the kale"
    body: "Chop the kale and massage it with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt for 1–2 minutes, until softened and slightly darker in colour."
  - title: "Cook the protein"
    body: "Cube the tofu and season with cumin, turmeric, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and olive oil. If using chickpeas, season them the same way. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for about 15 minutes, until lightly golden."
  - title: "Prep the vegetables"
    body: "Thinly slice the cabbage and red pepper (a mandolin works great here). Cube the cucumber and slice the avocado."
  - title: "Make the dressing"
    body: "Whisk together peanut butter, sesame oil, 2 tbsp olive oil, rice vinegar, lime juice, siracha, soy sauce , nutritional yeast(optional) and salt and pepper until smooth and creamy."
  - title: "Assemble"
    body: "Option 1: Toss everything together with the dressing. Top up with avocado slices. Option 2 (recommended): Build the bowl with quinoa and kale as the base, layer the vegetables and protein on top, and drizzle the dressing at the end for a more elevated look."
howToServe:
  - "Do not mix in the avocado as it will get mushy."
  - "Can be served immediately or stored in the fridge up to 24 hours (do not mix in the dressing if storing for later)."
  - "Can be served as a full meal, or a snack or appetizer with a lighter amount of protein."
chefsNote: "Ca substitute the vegetables with whatever you have on hand. Can add animal protein like ground beef on top, if not vegetarian. Great for meal prep- dress just before serving to keep it crisp."

steps:
  - title: ""
    body: "Prep the vegetables"
  - title: ""
    body: "Thinly slice the cabbage using a knife or mandolin. Julienne the carrots, cucumber, and pear into thin strips."
  - title: ""
    body: "Chop the herbs"
  - title: ""
    body: "Finely chop the green onion, dill, and cilantro."
  - title: ""
    body: "Roast the seeds"
  - title: ""
    body: "Lightly toast the pumpkin seeds in a dry pan over medium heat until golden and fragrant. Set aside to cool."
  - title: ""
    body: "Make the dressing"
  - title: ""
    body: "In a small bowl, whisk together pomegranate molasses, olive oil, balsamic glaze, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper until well combined."
  - title: ""
    body: "Assemble the slaw"
  - title: ""
    body: "In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, carrots, cucumber, pear, and herbs. Pour over the dressing and toss until everything is evenly coated."
  - title: ""
    body: "Finish and serve"
  - title: ""
    body: "Transfer to a serving bowl and top with feta cheese, pomegranate seeds, and roasted pumpkin seeds."

ingredients:
  - name: "Spaghetti Squash"
    amount: "1 large"
  - name: "Shredded Mozzarella"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Grated Halloumi"
    amount: "1 cup"
  - name: "Garlic"
    amount: "2 (medium) cloves"
  - name: "Mushroom"
    amount: "2 cups (chopped)"
  - name: "Kale"
    amount: "1 cup (chopped"
  - name: "Fresh Oregano"
    amount: "1 tbsp"
  - name: "Lime or Lemon juice"
    amount: "to taste"
  - name: "Olive Oil"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - name: "Salt and Pepper"
    amount: "to taste"
steps:
  - title: ""
    body: "Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)"
  - title: ""
    body: "Cut spaghetti squash lengthwise and brush both sides with olive oil. Season with salt & black pepper."
  - title: ""
    body: "Place flesh-side down, roast for 20 minutes."
  - title: ""
    body: "Remove from oven and gently separate the strands with a fork. Scoop out half of the strands, keeping the shells intact."
  - title: ""
    body: "Make the stuffing:"
  - title: ""
    body: "Sauté garlic in olive oil, add mushrooms + kale + oregano. Season with salt & pepper. Finish with lime juice."
  - title: ""
    body: "Fill the squash shells with the warm stuffing."
  - title: ""
    body: "Sprinkle mozzarella, layer back the remaining squash strands."
  - title: ""
    body: "Top with grated halloumi for crispy edges."
  - title: ""
    body: "Switch oven to BROIL at 450°F (230°C) and cook for 5 minutes until golden and crisp."
  - title: ""
    body: "Nooshe Jaan!"
howToServe:
  - "Pair with crusty sourdough or toasted crostini on the side."
  - "Use a wide, shallow plate or serve directly in the roasted squash shell for impact."
  - "Add a quick finish on top: a drizzle of warm olive oil, a sprinkle of lime zest, or a few fresh oregano leaves."
chefsNote: "Cutting lengthwise makes the squash strands longer and easier to pull. Swap stuffing for any red pasta sauce or veg-packed sauté you love. Best served immediately for peak crunch and pull."
